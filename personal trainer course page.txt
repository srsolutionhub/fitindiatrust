<h6>Why Personal Trainer Certification is Important?</h6>

<p class="text-justify">Earning a recognized certification will give you better opportunities in your fitness career. Personal Trainer certification provides additional and useful knowledge that boosts your chances of getting a good job or be a successful personal trainer. Benefits of this certification are:-</p>

<ul class="pt-15">
	<li>● Increase in practical skills and knowledge</li>
	<li>● Career advancement</li>
	<li>● A rise in competitive advantage and efficiency</li>
	<li>● Increase in professional credibility</li>
	<li>● Growth in earning potential</li>
</ul>
</div>

<div class="single-description pt-40">
<h6>Course Benefits and Career Opportunities.</h6>

<p class="text-justify">Key facilities that are available in this course are:-</p>

<ul class="pt-15">
	<li>● Batch Flexibility Options- We value your time; thus, we provide you with batch flexibility that lets you schedule batch location as per your availability.</li>
	<li>● Practical learning approach- We have a balanced mix of theoretical and practical teaching</li>
	<li>● Opportunity to interact with certified known faculties- Highly recognized, certified and experienced faculties are going to mentor and guide you.</li>
</ul>

<p class="text-justify">This course has amazing lifelong benefits as well, such as:-</p>

<ul class="pt-15">
	<li>● Lifetime placement assistance</li>
	<li>● Lifetime FIT Membership</li>
</ul>
</div>

<div class="single-description pt-40">
<h6>Why Apply for a Certified Personal Trainer Course?</h6>

<ul class="pt-15">
	<li>● As people are so busy these days they hardly have any time to go to a gym and have a workout session instead they look out for personal trainers who can help them reach their fitness goals at the comfort of their home or at a local gym nearby, which led to the demand of certified personal trainers who could train busy professionals and help them reach their fitness goals.</li>
	<li>● There is a lot of competition in the market among gym trainers to attract clients, thus it is required to apply for a certified personal trainer course to have better job opportunities and stay one step ahead of others.</li>
</ul>
</div>

<div class="single-description pt-40">
<h6>Where do we have our centers?</h6>

<ul class="pt-15">
	<li>● We have our centers in Delhi/NCR, you can apply for a certified personal trainer course in Delhi, Noida, and Gurugram. Fit India Trust is also working to create new centers in small cities of India to increase the employment power of the individuals and provide affordable education to gym trainers across India.</li>
</ul>
</div>

<div class="single-description pt-40">
<h6>Benefits of the FIT-CPT course?</h6>

<ul class="pt-15">
	<li>● There are numerous benefits of completing the FIT-CPT Course that include lifetime CPT certificate, placement assistance, and preparation of ACE Personal Trainer Certification Course which allows you to apply for ACE-CPT competitive exams which are accepted Internationally.</li>
</ul>

                    <div class="first-que">
                       <span class="que">Q. How do I become a certified Personal trainer in India?</span>
                      <p>Having a certification from a recognized organization is a prerequisite for entering the fitness industry. Once you've decided that becoming a personal trainer is right for you, the first step is choosing a certification program. Fit India Trust offers a variety of <a href="https://www.fitindiatrust.org/fitness-academy/fitness-trainer-certification">fitness training courses</a> with written and practical examinations.</p>
                    </div>
                     <div class="other-que">
                    <span class="que">Q. What are the benefits of a Certified Personal trainer?</span>
                    <p>Having your personal training certification means you have learned the fundamentals of fitness, movement, exercise science, training, and coaching. Trainer certification also proves you have the knowledge base necessary to help clients meet their goals safely and effectively.
-Whether you are offering one-to-one training, boot camps, or aerobics classes, you control what you earn.
-Controlling your earnings and your hours, you become your boss as a personal trainer.</p>
 </div>
 <div class="other-que">
 <span class="que">Q. How is Fit India Trust different from any other fitness training institute in India?</span>
<p>We cannot compare Fit India Trust with other institutes as this would be unfair to comment upon Fit India Trust and other institute's effort. We can say that Fit India Trust has rapidly covered most parts of the country in a concise period by constantly providing the fitness industry with well-educated exercise science professionals. On the other side, Fit India Trust courses are budget-friendly as we understand the monetary factors involved and the financial struggles which our students undergo while pursuing any course.</p>
        </div>     <div class="other-que">     
                  <span class="que">Q.If I don't clear examinations, will I get a certificate?</span>
<p>To maintain and preserve the reputation of the Fit India Trust certifications and ensure that employers across the country trust the accreditation, we do not offer any participation certificate that a candidate could misuse to pretend that they have passed our examinations. Successful completion of all exams is compulsory for certification in all the courses offered by Fit India Trust.
</p></div> <div class="other-que">
 <span class="que">Q. Can I pay the course fees in parts?</span>
<p>Yes, the fees for the courses at Fit India trust can be paid in 3 Easy Installments without any additional charges.
Do get in touch with our Course Counselor by calling on 7999567519 for more information on the course fees and payment options.
 </p>
